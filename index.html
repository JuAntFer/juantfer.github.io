About Me

I specialize in designing efficient deep learning models for deployment on embedded systems and resource-constrained environments. My work focuses on model compression, including quantization, pruning, and structural reparameterization, to enable real-world AI applications in areas like autonomous driving, healthcare, and smart devices.

I hold a dual Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology from TUM (Germany) and ENSEA (France), with an exchange semester at KAIST (South Korea), where I focused on AI and robotics.
